Biking BC


Cycling has always been one of my passions, and now I live in one of the most important
places in mountain biking's history.

The hills here are awesome, supper steep with a mixture of every type of terrain imaginable.
It also has some of the most amazing rock slabs around.

And the best part is you get to be surrounded by wonderful panoramas
like the one shown at the top of this page.
